My Study Abroad Experience

My summer abroad in Europe was truly an amazing experience. I took a course at London School of Economics, Real Estate Finance. I really enjoyed the class, it was stuff I found very interesting to learn about. I also really liked the school and teaching structure. However, since it was a summer class, we had 3 hour long classes every day and a recitation after, so it was a little draining.

My friend and I did our best to maximize our experiences while prioritizing our class as well. We went to London about 2 weeks before our class started, and travelled to Florence and Barcelona and spent some time exploring London.

Florence was so beautiful, we saw so many beautiful sights, ate authentic Italian food at delicious restaurants, and took a lot of pictures. In Barcelona, we travelled to a beautiful big beach and saw one of a kind sights. We went to other beaches as well and had a great time. The environment was so fun and free and I could see myself visiting again for sure.

This experience has made me fall in love with travelling and I want to visit a lot of places, including Morocco, Greece, Paris, Thailand, Amsterdam, South Korea, Austria, Sri Lanka, and more.
